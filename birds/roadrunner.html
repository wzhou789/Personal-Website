Roadrunner

The Greater Roadrunner are fast-running ground cuckoos found in the southwestern and south-central US and Mexico. They can outrace humans and have been clocked at up to 27mph. They are large and slender with long legs, long tails, crests, and an oversized dark bill. They can eat poisonous prey including venomous lizards, scorpions, and rattlesnakes.
