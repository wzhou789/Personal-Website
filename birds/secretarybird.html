Secretary Bird

Secretary birds are large terrestrial birds of prey native to Africa. It has an eagle-like body on crane-like legs, coming up to a height of around 1.3 meters. The adults have a featherless red-orange face, grey plummage, and black flight feathers and thighs. Their prey mainly consist of insects, mammals, and other small animals. There are occasional reports of them killing young gazelles and cheetah cubs. They mainly hunt by scaring targets before chasing them and finally killing them with strikes from their feet. Their population has experienced a rapid decline and is classed as an endangered species.
